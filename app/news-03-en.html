Visit of Richard James Glassey

Professor of School of Computing Science and Digital Media, Robert Gordon University (UK) Richard James Glassey is visiting KBTU from June 1 to June 16, 2013. Richard Glassey is a Ph.D. in Computer Science (University of Strathclyde).

He has a professional experience in the field of multimedia technology and computer graphics, and he is a Fellow of the Higher Education academy (UK).

During his visit Dr. Richard Glassey will conduct modular course "Advanced Programming in Web Development" for Multimedia Technologies master students.

He has more than 10 research publications in the field of Information Technologies.
