Процедура поступления

Innovative education services

Application:

  • — Application request to the Rector of KBTU;
  • — Notarized copy of  diploma of higher education with transcripts;
  • — Foreign applicants are required to submit a notarized copy of diploma  and transcript of credits translated into - Kazakh or Russian;
  • — Resume;
  • — One reference letter;
  • — 6 photos (3x4cm);
  • — Copy of identity card;
  • — Health certificate (on Form No.086-校);
  • — Copy of a work book or letter of employment verification
  • — Copy of TIN (taxpayer identification number)
  • — Copy of a valid TOEFL IbT/TOEFL/IELTS (if available)

Payment options

30% of the tuition fee advance payment before the start of the academic year;

30% of the tuition fee to be paid before 1 December of the current academic year;

40% of the tuition fee to be paid before 1 April of the current academic year
